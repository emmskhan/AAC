Quickschools

All parents and students (3rd and above) will have access to Quickschools Platform. They will be assigned a username and password during the first few weeks of school. You can use their credentials to view student grades, attendance, and the updated school calendar.

Student Google Accounts

All students in Annoor Academy have access to the G Suite for Education platform. Education-class G Suite accounts grant access to Google apps and services like Drive, Classroom, and Sites, as well as resources like Sora.

Student Email

Students have access to Gmail for collaboration with teachers and other students. To keep student Gmail safe and productive, email communication is restricted to approved sites only. We also filter email for explicit content and abusive behavior.

Student Sign-In to Google Accounts

Google Classroom

Annoor academy teachers will be using Google Classroom as a learning management system from August 2023. All assignments, announcements, assessments, and other learning information will be posted by each teacher in their respective Google Classrooms. Students can only access Google Classroom through their school Google accounts. All parents will have access to their child/children's classroom.
