Google Classroom

Annoor academy teachers will be using Google Classroom as a learning management system from August 2023. All assignments, announcements, assessments, and other learning information will be posted by each teacher in his/her respective Google Classroom. Students can only access Google Classroom through their school Gmail accounts. All parents will have access to their child/children's classroom as guadians.
